GIANG LAM, CEO

GIANG LAM

Giang is an entrepreneur and investor with over 25 years of experience, including over 13 years in Vietnam. He is Founder of the RDA Vietnam Fund, a Boston-based fund investing in Vietnam. Prior to managing investments, Giang was a successful entrepreneur having co-founded several high-growth companies including Cambridge Energy and JungleSoft. Giang has held operational roles in diverse industries including mobile, telecom, robotics, manufacturing, and healthcare. Giang received his BS in Mechanical Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and his MS in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Maryland at College Park. Giang is fluent in both Vietnamese and English.
